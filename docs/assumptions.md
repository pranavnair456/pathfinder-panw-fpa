# Assumptions & Sources

> **All data in this project is SYNTHETIC.** It is *calibrated* to PANW's publicly disclosed
> figures so the analytics are credible, but no confidential or proprietary PANW data is used.
> Every customer, event, and GL row is generated by `data/generate.py` with a fixed seed.
> Real anchor figures and their sources are catalogued below. Where PANW does not disclose a
> figure (e.g. per-platform ARR), the modeled allocation is flagged **[MODELED]**.

PANW fiscal year ends **July 31**. FY quarters: Q1 ≈ Aug–Oct, Q2 ≈ Nov–Jan, Q3 ≈ Feb–Apr, Q4 ≈ May–Jul.

## 8. [MODELED] allocations (not disclosed by PANW)

- **Per-platform NGS ARR split** (Strata / Prisma Cloud / Cortex-XSIAM / Identity / Observability):
  PANW reports NGS ARR only in aggregate (plus selective call-outs: SASE ARR +35%, ~6,300 SASE
  customers; AI ARR ~$545M in Q4 FY25, +2.5×). We allocate the real NGS ARR totals across platforms
  using a realistic, documented mix — Strata largest and slowest; Prisma Cloud + Cortex fastest —
  with Identity (CyberArk) and Observability (Chronosphere) appearing as **inorganic step-changes**
  at their close dates. The per-platform series **sum to the real NGS ARR totals**.
- **Product vs subscription split for older quarters**: anchored to the exact Q3 FY26 split
  ($594M product / $2,408M subscription) and PANW's well-known trend of a declining hardware share.
- **Customer-level ledger, cohorts, threat signals, plan/budget**: fully synthetic, seeded.
