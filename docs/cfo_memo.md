# CFO Memo — Q3 FY2026 NGS ARR Review

**To:** CFO
**From:** FP&A (Pathfinder)
**Re:** Q3 FY2026 Next-Gen Security ARR — what the headline hides, and where to lean in
*(All figures synthetic, calibrated to public disclosures — illustrative of the analysis.)*

## Bottom line
NGS ARR reached **~$8.1B, +60% YoY** — a record — and came in **~4% ahead of plan**. But **~$1.6B of
that growth is inorganic** (CyberArk + Chronosphere). **Organic NGS ARR grew ~28%**, which is the
number that should anchor our internal scorecard and external credibility. The quarter is strong,
but the *mix* is shifting and the FY2030 target now requires above-trend organic execution.

## What drove the quarter
- **Expansion, not new logos, led the beat.** The ARR bridge shows existing-customer expansion as
  the largest positive motion, with churn contained — the platformization flywheel working as
  designed. Net revenue retention held at **~120%**, and platformized customers retained/expanded at
  **~135% vs ~114%** for single-product accounts.
- **Inorganic mix is now ~20% of NGS ARR.** Healthy strategically (identity + observability
  adjacencies) but it flatters the headline growth rate. We should lead external communication with
  the organic figure to protect credibility.

## The watch-item
Organic growth is **decelerating** (high-50s% → ~28%). Extrapolating today's trend, our base-case
model reaches only **~$15B by FY2030 (76% of the $20B target)**; the bull case reaches ~$18B. Closing
the gap implies sustaining a **~23% CAGR** — achievable, but not on autopilot.

## Recommendations
1. **Fund platformization aggressively.** The incentive ROI is compelling — illustrative campaigns
   show **strong NPV, IRR well above hurdle, and ~1–2 year payback**, driven almost entirely by the
   NRR gap. This is our highest-confidence organic lever; protect/expand the budget.
2. **Hold the line on organic disclosure.** Report organic NGS ARR prominently so the inorganic
   step-ups don't mask the underlying trend (either direction).
3. **Treat the $20B target as execution-contingent.** Platformization alone can't close the gap
   (our solver shows it would take more conversions than the 4,000 target). Pair it with sustained
   new-logo growth and a disciplined, NPV-positive M&A cadence — accepting that the best tuck-ins may
   be **EPS-dilutive in year 1 but value-accretive** over time.

**Ask:** approve continued platformization incentive spend at current+ levels and adopt
organic-NGS-ARR as the primary internal growth KPI for FY2027 planning.
